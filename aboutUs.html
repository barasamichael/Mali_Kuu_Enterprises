Welcome to our GUI application, designed to help you manage your stock, suppliers, customers, and sales with ease. This Java-based application allows you to register your customers and suppliers, track their transactions, and manage your inventory all in one place.
With our application, you can easily add, edit, or delete customer and supplier information, as well as add, edit, or delete products and items to your stock. You can also create and track supplies made by your suppliers and orders placed by your customers, with the ability to view and modify their details.
Our application displays all data in a clear and organized tabular format, making it easy for you to quickly access and analyze information. We use a MySQL database for secure and reliable data storage, ensuring that all your information is safe and easily retrievable.
Our objectives for this project include:

  1. Providing an easy-to-use and intuitive interface for managing your business's stock, suppliers, customers, and sales.
  2. Ensuring that all data is securely stored and easily accessible.
  3. Creating a system that streamlines your business processes and saves you time and effort.

As with any project, there is always room for improvement. Some possible areas for improvement include:
  1. Adding more features and functionality to the application, such as the ability to generate reports and track sales trends over time.
  2. Improving the application's performance and speed, especially when dealing with large amounts of data.
  3. Incorporating more user feedback and suggestions to make the application more tailored to individual business needs.

Overall, our GUI application is designed to make managing your business's stock, suppliers, customers, and sales as easy and efficient as possible. We hope that our application meets your needs and helps you to achieve your business objectives.
